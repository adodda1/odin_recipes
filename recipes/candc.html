Cookies&Cream Milkshake

Description

Recreate your favorite Chick Fil A Milkshake in just 5 minutes, with 4 ingredients, using this easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • 6 oreo cookies
  • 2 1/2 cups french vanilla ice cream, softened
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  1. In a blender, crush cookies with a few good pulses.
  2. Add ice cream, milk and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into 2 tall glasses and garnish.
